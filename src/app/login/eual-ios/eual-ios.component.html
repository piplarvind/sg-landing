The SportGrit Volleyball App provides a platform for volleyball clubs and its coaches, athletes, and parents to co-create an ecosystem of collaboration to prepare teams and athletes for success. The app also allows college recruiters to lookup athletes, view game stats, and follow athletes to monitor their training, competition results, game highlight videos, and coordinate with club coaches about athletes in whom they may have recruiting interest.

 

Additionally, the SportGrit Volleyball App adds convenience and cost savings for clubs by providing automated registering for tryouts, coaching clinics, and other club-specific events, a communication portal (email to the entire club, individual teams, players, parents), automatic and recurring payments by parents or athletes for their monthly or annual club fees, and even platforms to gather information from uniform sizing to player recruiting info.

When a user signs in to the SportGrit Volleyball App after initial onboarding, they return to the user portal of the club they initially selected and in which they most recently on-boarded. The user portal allows visibility to teams, players, stats, leader boards, schedules, events, and more.
