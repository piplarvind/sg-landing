Account Deletion Process

1. Navigate to the "Menu" in the bottom tab of the app.
2. Inside the Menu, locate and tap on the "Settings" button.
3. Within the Settings, find the "Delete Account" option.
4. Upon confirmation, users will be navigated to a screen detailing the data that will be deleted.
5. This screen will display a summary of the information to be removed, including posts on feeds, personal details, and progress within the app.
6. Users will have the opportunity to review this information before proceeding.
7. Upon confirming the deletion, a bottom sheet will appear with a final confirmation.
8. The bottom sheet will include a "Yes, Delete My Account" button for users to click and proceed.
9. Users may also have the option to cancel the deletion if they change their mind.
10. Once the "Yes" button is clicked, the account will be permanently deleted, and users will be logged out of the app.
11. Note: Transaction history will be retained for record-keeping purposes, ensuring transparency in financial transactions.
